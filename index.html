Kombucha

Kombucha is a fermented tea drink

About:

Kombucha is a tea drink that has been around 200+ years, this drink goes through a fermentation process. This fermentation process occurs when you introduce SCOBY into a tea drink and let it sit in the tea for a couple weeks or months. SCOBY is a living culture of bacteria and yeast. Kombucha includes this culture of bacteria and yeast from the SCOBY, which is great for probiotic intake! Probiotics help with digestive issues, IBS, and helps strengthen your immune system. It is important to have a proper intake of probiotics, Kombucha is a great way to get probiotics in as it is quick, convienent and can be consumed on the go.
